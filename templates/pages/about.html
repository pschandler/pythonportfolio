about this site

Mission Statement

This site will demonstrate proficient and competent knowledge of the python programming language by providing practical examples in many different areas. Wherever possible, examples will be showcased directly in the browser, however some applications, such as desktop apps, data science projects and other command-line applications will link to a github location for closer examination.

Inspiration and Guidance

The projects demonstrated in this site were inspired, if not taken directly from, a course titled "100 Days of Code: The Complete Python Pro Bootcamp" led by instructor Dr. Angela Yu. This course comes highly recommended by punchcodestudios and can be found here on the Udemy.com site. Thank you, Angela for your inspiration and for sharing your enormous wealth of knowledge on this fascinating subject!
